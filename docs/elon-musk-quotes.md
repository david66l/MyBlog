# 埃隆·马斯克名言集

> 整理自公开演讲与访谈。英文原文 + 中文对照。

---

## 坚持与行动

> When something is important enough, you do it even if the odds are not in your favor.

当一件事足够重要时，即使胜算不高，你也要去做。

> Persistence is very important. You should not give up unless you are forced to give up.

坚持非常重要。除非被逼无奈，否则绝不放弃。

> No, I don't ever give up. I'd have to be dead or completely incapacitated.

不，我从不放弃。除非我死了或完全失去行动能力。

---

## 创新与失败

> Failure is an option here. If things are not failing, you are not innovating enough.

失败是一种选择。如果没有失败，说明你创新得不够。

> The first step is to establish that something is possible, then probability will occur.

第一步是先相信一件事是可能的，然后可能性才会发生。

> You should take the approach that you're wrong. Your goal is to be less wrong.

你要假设自己是错的，目标是少犯错误。

---

## 工作与努力

> Work like hell. I mean you just have to put in 80 to 100 hour weeks every week.

像地狱一样工作。每周要投入 80 到 100 小时。

> You get paid in direct proportion to the difficulty of problems you solve.

你的收入与你解决问题的难度成正比。

---

## 人生与未来

> It is possible for ordinary people to choose to be extraordinary.

普通人也可以选择变得非凡。

> Life needs to be more than just solving problems every day. You need to wake up and be excited about the future.

生活不能只是每天解决问题。你要醒来时对未来充满兴奋。

> Some people don't like change, but you need to embrace change if the alternative is disaster.

有些人不喜欢改变，但如果不改变就是灾难，那你就必须拥抱改变。

---

## 其他经典语录

> Constantly seek criticism. A well thought out critique of whatever you're doing is as valuable as gold.

不断寻求批评。对你所做事情的深思熟虑的批评，如同黄金般珍贵。

> Question every requirement.

质疑每一个需求。（第一性原理思考的核心）

> I could either watch it happen or be a part of it.

我可以坐视它发生，也可以参与其中。

> Good ideas are always crazy until they're not.

好主意在实现之前总是看起来很疯狂。
